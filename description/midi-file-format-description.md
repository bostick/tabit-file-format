MIDI files have a header and multiple tracks.

The header is a chunk:
```
M T h d . . . . . . . . . .         4d 54 68 64 00 00 00 06 00 01 00 02 00 c0
```

This is a better visualization of the header:
```
M T h d                             4d 54 68 64 ; type
. . . .                             00 00 00 06 ; length
. .                                 00 01       ; format
. .                                 00 02       ; track count
. .                                 00 c0       ; division
```

Track 0 is a chunk:
```
M T r k . . . . . . X . . . . .     4d 54 72 6b 00 00 00 13 00 ff 58 04 04 02 18 08
. . Q . . .   . . / .               00 ff 51 03 07 a1 20 00 ff 2f 00
```
